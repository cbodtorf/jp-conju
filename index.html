jp-conju.now.sh

Microservice utility to conjugate Japanese verbs

usage

            
              const verb = '建てる'
              const verbtype = 'v1'
              fetch(`https://jp-conju.now.sh/api/conju?verb=${verb}&verbtype=${verbtype}`)

endpoints

https://jp-conju.now.sh/api/conju?verb={verb}&verbtype={verbtype}
 
https://jp-conju.now.sh/api/conju?verb={verb}
